Thank you very much for your interest in our trial, “DIGEST". We are excited to have you involved! Please read this email carefully as it includes important information and next steps.

 

The purpose of this trial is to assess the effectiveness of the Habitual total diet replacement programme in achieving reversal (also referred to as remission) in people with Type 2 diabetes. The trial will be conducted entirely remotely.

 

If you agree to participate, you will be supported by the research team at Lindus Health. You can learn more about us here. 

 

Please review the copy of the Participant Information Sheet (PIS) attached to this email. This document provides all the details about the aims and objectives of the trial and what is required of you. Please take the time to read this document carefully and pay close attention to inclusion and exclusion criteria.

 

If you believe you are eligible for the trial and wish to proceed, then the next step is to follow the link below to book a time slot for a screening appointment with a member of our research staff. This will be a video call during which the trial details will be explained and you will have a chance to ask any questions that you might have. After this, if you wish to proceed with your participation, we will ask you to give us your informed consent.

 

Book a screening call

 

Once that’s done, we will use specialised software to randomly allocate you to one of the two trial groups:

 

Standard Care Group: 

  • You will continue to receive standard routine care with your GP.

HHabitual Programme Group:

  • You will receive the Habitual remission programme.
  • This will involve total diet replacement for the first 3 months, then diet re-introduction for a further 3 months.
  • You will be supported throughout the trial with the Habitual digital therapeutic mobile app.

If you have any questions, please let us know by replying to this email or calling us on 0800 3687989.

Thank you again for your interest!
